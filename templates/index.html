Data

Three Urban Driving Datasets

One is synthetic and the others are realistic

GTA

A dataset of 24,966 labeled synthetic images, generated from the eponymous video game.

Cityscapes

Contains 25,000 labeled urban scenes from cities around Germany, 5,000 are fine annotated and others are coarse.

Mapillary

A dataset collected in multiple cities around the world, which is composed of 20,000 labeled scenes.

Our Models

ADVENT (Baseline)
ADVENT address the task of unsupervised domain adaptation in semantic segmentation with losses based on the entropy of the pixel-wise predictions

MTKT (Multi-Teatcher Knowledge Transfer)
MTKT proposes the multitarget knowledge transfer (MTKT) framework, with novel network design and learning scheme which do not rely on the joint minimization of multiple adversarial losses over the same classifier module, hopefully reducing the instability of the training.
